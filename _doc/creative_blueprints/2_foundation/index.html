Scattered Tools, Fragmented Data

The Problem

Engineering and product teams struggle with fragmented tooling. Project managers track in Jira, designers collaborate in Figma, engineers push to GitHub, and status updates live in Slack. Critical dependencies get missed, timelines slip, and teams lose weeks to re-alignment and context-switching.

The Solution

Teams waste hours context-switching between disconnected tools, losing visibility into dependencies, and failing to coordinate across sprints and initiatives.

Target Audience

Engineering leaders, product managers, and scrum teams seeking better coordination

Personal Motivation

Enable teams to focus on shipping, not coordination overhead.
